Title: Hedda Gabler

Author: Henrik Ibsen

MRS. ELVSTED.

[Looks at her watch.]

It's past seven.

HEDDA.

When did Tesman come home?

MRS. ELVSTED.

He has not come.

HEDDA.

Not come home yet?

MRS. ELVSTED.

[Rising.]

No one has come.

HEDDA.

Think of our watching and waiting here till four in the morning--

MRS. ELVSTED.

[Wringing her hands.]

And how I watched and waited for him!
